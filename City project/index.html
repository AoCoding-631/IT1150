Mount Ararat and the Yerevan skyline Straddling the divide between Asia and Europe, Armenia might be one of the final travel frontiers left on the planet for adventurous backpackers. From its snowy mountain peaks in the Caucasus region to a large number of perfectly preserved medieval monasteries, visiting Armenia isn’t often on a traveler’s to-do list.
